Maestro Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Maestro is a music controller app for ceremony DJs. This policy describes how Maestro handles your information.

Data We Collect

Maestro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no user accounts.

Apple Music

Maestro uses Apple's MusicKit framework to access your Apple Music library and control playback. Any data exchanged between Maestro and Apple Music is handled directly by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. Maestro itself does not see, store, or transmit your music library, listening history, or Apple ID information.

Permissions

Maestro requests permission to access Apple Music for the sole purpose of playing songs you select within the app. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings.

Children

Maestro is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone of any age.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will appear at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
