Common Questions

Maestro can't find my music.
Maestro uses Apple Music via MusicKit. You need an active Apple Music subscription and must grant Maestro permission to access your library on first launch. To re-enable: iOS Settings → Maestro → toggle Media & Apple Music on.

Songs won't play.
Confirm your Apple Music subscription is active and that you have an internet connection. Songs not available for streaming in your region cannot be played.

The crossfade isn't smooth.
Crossfade depends on Apple Music being able to pre-buffer the next song. A weak connection can cause brief drops. Use Wi-Fi or strong cellular for live ceremonies.

Requesting a feature.
Email me with what you'd like. Maestro is built for ceremony work — if there's a workflow you'd like better support for, I want to hear about it.
